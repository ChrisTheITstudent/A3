Skills and Jobs

Project Manager

The Project Manager will report to the Project Focus executive and will manage a multidisciplinary team to develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance the Care Focus software application. The Project Manager will possess excellent stakeholder management and communication skills and will be responsible for developing all project artifacts associated with the project.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain project artifacts including the Project Plan, schedule and registers.
  • Track project progress and allocate tasks through an electronic workflow tool.
  • Provide regular progress updates to the executive, immediately highlighting any change requirements, risks and issues that become evident.
  • Manage the project team to deliver a guaranteed minimum viable product within constraints including timeframe and budget.

Skills and Experience

  • Experience in managing a high functioning agile development team is essential.
  • A proven ability to manage budgets, expenditure, forecasting and associated reporting.

Qualifications

  • Project Management certification is essential - AGILE preferred.
  • Other relevant tertiary IT qualifications or private certifications would be highly regarded.

Software Developer - PowerApps

The Software Developer will report to the Project Manager and will work collaboratively with other team members to develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance the Care Focus software application. The successful candidate will ideally have a minimum of three years’ experience in developing bespoke applications using Microsoft Power Apps – particularly Power Automate - and will be experienced in working as part of a high performing multidisciplinary agile development team. The ability to manage tasks via electronic workflow and to work autonomously is essential.

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, maintain and enhance the Care Focus software application in an agile development environment according to project requirements.
  • Contribute as a member of the development team to the identification and development of tasks according to requirements.
  • Assist in the gathering of functional requirements and translate into technical specifications.

Skills and Experience

  • Excellent communication skills including the ability to liaise appropriately with stakeholders and team members throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Proficiency in the Microsoft Azure PowerApps service, including integration with Microsoft 365 products including SharePoint and Teams.
  • Experience working as part of a high functioning agile development team is essential.

Qualifications

  • Relevant tertiary qualifications or private certifications would be highly regarded.
